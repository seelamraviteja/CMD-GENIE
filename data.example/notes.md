# Personal notes

Extra facts the genie should know that aren't in your command cheat-sheets —
IPs, hostnames, URLs, accounts. One fact per line is plenty.

The genie answers fact questions from here **and** from your cheat-sheets
(commands.md, quick-access.md). If a fact isn't written down anywhere, it says
"Not available" instead of guessing.

## examples
- Jumpbox IP: 10.0.0.5
- Staging URL: https://staging.example.com
